## üìå Unit 1: Quantum Mechanics (Solved)
### Q1. Derive Time-Independent Schr√∂dinger Wave Equation. (7 Marks)
**Answer Key:**
1.  Total Energy $E = \text{Kinetic} (K) + \text{Potential} (V) = \frac{p^2}{2m} + V$.
2.  Wave function: $\Psi = A e^{-i\omega t}$.
3.  Differentiate $\Psi$ twice w.r.t $x$: $\frac{d^2\Psi}{dx^2} = -k^2 \Psi = -\frac{4\pi^2}{\lambda^2}\Psi$.
4.  Use De-Broglie $\lambda = h/p$: $\frac{d^2\Psi}{dx^2} = -\frac{p^2}{\hbar^2}\Psi$.
5.  Substitute $p^2 = 2m(E-V)$:
    *   **$\nabla^2\Psi + \frac{2m}{\hbar^2}(E - V)\Psi = 0$**

### Q2. Compton Effect & Shift Formula. (7 Marks)
**Answer Key:**
*   **Definition:** Increase in wavelength of X-rays when scattered by free electrons.
*   **Formula:** $\Delta \lambda = \frac{h}{m_0 c}(1 - \cos \theta)$.
*   Max shift at $\theta = 180^\circ$ ($\Delta \lambda = 0.0484 \mathring{A}$).

### Q3. Particle in 1D Box. (7 Marks)
*   Potential $V=0$ inside, $\infty$ outside.
*   Boundary Conditions: $\Psi(0)=0, \Psi(L)=0$.
*   **Result:** $E_n = \frac{n^2 h^2}{8mL^2}$.

1.  $\nabla \cdot D = \rho$ (Gauss Electrostatics)
